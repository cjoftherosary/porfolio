About Me

Hi, I'm Rio.

I chose a program I don't fit in. My outputs were too metaphorical to be considered as good reporting, and too precise to be considered as good prose. The rules of thumb of journalism handed me a splinter of ambiguity.

I dislike vagueness, often frowning because of it until it ushers me to a hyperfixated need to learn. I operate the way kittens do, mimicking my mentors the way kittens mimic their mothers. I think the felines are onto something with this one. Do you like cats?
